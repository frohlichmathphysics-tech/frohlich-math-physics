Refraction threshold — light going from water into air

Mr. Frohlich Math and Physics · IB Physics SL — Topic C.3
Laser

How to use this applet

  1. Aim the laser. Drag the laser pen along its arc to change the angle of incidence (0°–80° from the normal). Inside the dish is water; below the flat face is air.
  2. Watch the rays. Note what happens to the refracted ray (in air, below the flat face) and the reflected ray (back into the water) as you sweep through angles.
  3. Try all three colours. Each colour is a different wavelength of light. Use the colour buttons above the canvas to switch.
