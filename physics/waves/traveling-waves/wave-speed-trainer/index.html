WAVE SPEED TRAINER
SPEED:
Pulse the wave from the source, time it from marker A to marker B with the stopwatch, and measure the distance between the markers with the ruler. The wave speed is c = Δd / t. The stopwatch reads wall-clock seconds; at ¼× speed the pulse takes 4.0 s on the wall clock to travel 1.0 s in the scene — divide your stopwatch reading by the speed multiplier to get scene-time.
