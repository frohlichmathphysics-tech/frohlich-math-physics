Three climate stations. Three padlocks. Solve them in any order — the door opens only when all three agree.

Station Alpha · Temperature
Kerala daily April temperature
μ = 33°C · σ = 2°C · n = 400 days
Approximately how many of the 400 days had a temperature above 37°C?
A note from Kai
How many standard deviations above the mean is 37 degrees? That tells you which slice of the 400 days you need.
Station Beta · Soil

Station Gamma · Grain
Sahel millet yield

Vault Custodian

The vault’s own air. Relative humidity is normally distributed over the monitoring period. A dehumidifier has three fan speeds — tell the Curator how often each must wake.

μ = 45% · σ = 5% · n = 10,000 hours

Dial 1 · Low fan
Low runs when humidity is between μ − σ and μ + σ. How many of the 10,000 hours did Low run?
Dial 2 · Medium fan
Medium runs when humidity is between μ + σ and μ + 2σ. How many hours did Medium run?
Dial 3 · High fan
High runs when humidity exceeds μ + 3σ. How many hours did High run?
A note from Kai
Each fan’s band is measured in whole standard deviations. Name the band in standard deviations first, then the rule gives the share.
