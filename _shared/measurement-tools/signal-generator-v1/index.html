What to test:
  • Body drag: click anywhere on the panel outside the sliders and power toggle, drag. Panel should follow your cursor inside the dark frame.
  • Frequency slider: drag the thumb left/right. The white fiducial line on the thumb is what the student reads against the tick scale above. Reading uncertainty is ±0.25 Hz (half the 0.5 Hz minor tick spacing). The fiducial should align with the scale visually as you drag.
  • Click-to-jump: click anywhere on the frequency track (not on the thumb). Thumb should jump to that x position. Continue dragging from there.
  • Amplitude slider: same drag and click-to-jump behavior. No ticks, no readout — pure gradient bar.
  • Power toggle: click the red OFF button. Should turn green and read ON. Click again → red OFF. The frequency and amplitude values should NOT change when toggling power.
  • Live readout (right panel): watch getFrequency(), getAmplitude(), and isOn() update as you interact. getReadingUncertainty() stays constant at ±0.25 Hz.
  • Event log (right panel, below readout): every change should append a line. Verify onChange fires for all three inputs.
  • Touch: open this page on a phone. Body drag, slider drag, click-to-jump, and power toggle should all work via touch.
  • Idle: leave the page idle for 60 seconds. No flicker, no console errors, panel state stable.
  • Rapid input: drag the frequency slider rapidly back and forth for ~10 seconds. No leaks, no freezes, thumb stays in sync with pointer.
