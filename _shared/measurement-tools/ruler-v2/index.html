What to test:
  • Horizontal ruler (left side): drag anywhere in the dark frame. Reading edge is the bottom edge. Slide over each blue reference shape and verify the reading matches the labeled true length (within ~0.5 cm).
  • Vertical ruler (right side): drag anywhere in the dark frame. Reading edge is the right edge. 0 is at the top, 100 at the bottom. Slide the right edge against the vertical blue bar and verify both end-positions match the labeled true length.
  • Numerals: 0, 10, 20, …, 100 at every 10 cm. Bare ticks between numerals every 1 cm; shorter half-ticks every 0.5 cm.
  • Drag both rulers off-frame on each side — at least a thin sliver of each body must stay visible so you can grab it back.
  • Mobile check (required before redeploy per Build_Rules v25 §0.1 mobile-testing lock): open this page on a phone, drag both rulers via touch, verify they respond and that the frame fits the viewport without horizontal scrolling.
